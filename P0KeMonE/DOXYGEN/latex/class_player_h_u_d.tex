\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{class_player_h_u_d}{Player\+HUD} class provides a heads-\/up display (HUD) for the player in the game. 

This class extends QGraphics\+Scene and is responsible for displaying the player\textquotesingle{}s team of Pokémon, their health, and handling the selection of Pokémon during gameplay.
